About Us.

A Fisherman's Life started when a young man decided to build a website for a project in school. That young man went on to make his website dreams come true, and eventually: A Fisherman's Life was born! We are a fishing services company based out of sunny Clearwater, Florida. Our staff is extremely passionate about fishing and the open water. We can assist you in your fishing adventure in a variety of ways:
  • Rent one of the expertly maintenanced boats from our fleet.
  • Rent some top of the line fishing gear.
  • Charter a fishing trip with some of our professional guides.
  • New to fishing? Take a class led by one of our pros!
